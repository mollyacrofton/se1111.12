About The Corpse Bride

A young man who is going to get married worries about the ceremony. He then ends up In the woods practing for the ceremony. Little did he know the branch he practice putting the ring on was, Emily's finger.The corpse bride. She then comes alive to pull him beneath the surface to seem some interesting people. He learns alot about her life and the place she come from.
